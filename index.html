About Me




I live in Fishtown, Philadelphia, and hold a Master's degree in UX Design from Jefferson University. After achieving this milestone, I wanted to advance my studies by learning more about how technology gets built. This lead to me enrolling in an immersive coding boot camp at Penn LPS.

I've always loved coding because it agrees with my analytical mindset and satisfies my craving to solve problems using technology. There is nothing more gratifying for me than fixing a line of code and getting something working.

I hope to land a developer role when I graduate in September, where I'll get to fully immerse myself in building technology products with my design sensibility. Please feel welcome to browse my work!
